Job Opportunities in Australia is Still Positive

The job opportunities and scope has increased in Australia across different states and territories in the last quarter of the year. At now, advanced industries across the Oz are looking to hire the employees having skills in high demand occupations.

A recent report from Manpower group says that overall job opportunities across Australia are good. However, the individuals looking for the job in the Kangaroo Land must do a thorough research, as the job options are highly diverse for different occupations across the states and territories.

There are various job sectors, which offer great scope to the skilled and talented professionals, i.e. Construction and mining, education, public administration, etc. All these sectors offer great job ratio, even higher than the national average along with the year over year improvements.

Although, some decline seen in the job prospects in some sectors, i.e. manufacturing, service, etc. however, overall outlook remained positive across different states and territories.

As per the statement from the Manpower group Oz and NZ, the employers in Australia are indicating their interest and need for the people with skills and talent. This positive intent towards hiring, among the Aussie employers has been there for almost a decade now.

State wise job prospects

As already mentioned that different state and territories show the variety in terms of job prospects, i.e. Construction and Mining are showing signs of great increase in outlook compared to other territories. Small territories and states are also showing great signs in terms of job prospects.

Western Australia has shown the sign of steady and consistent growth in the prospects compared to other states. South Australia, Tasmania and Queensland states of the Oz are not far behind with four percent enhancement compared to last year. Although, a marginal decline is noticed in the states, i.e. New South Wales, ACT and Victoria, however, overall prospects remains positive.

How to apply for Visa in Australia to find job

If you are looking to apply for relevant visa in Australia, the Skilled Independent Subclass 189 Visa is the ideal visa category to apply. It is a point tested visa under General Skilled Migration (GSM) category. You may choose the relevant occupation from the Australian Skilled Occupation List and subsequently, submit the EOI (Expression of Interest) in the online SkillSelect system of Australia. You need to obtain minimum 60 points to qualify as applicant based on your age, education, English language proficiency, work experience, etc. factors.

If you are selected based on your total collected points, you will receive Invitation to Apply (ITA) for the Subclass 189 Visa. You may gather the documents needed and submit the visa application to the immigration office.
